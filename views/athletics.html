2018-2019 Season

Season Recap:

Women's tennis ties school record for wins during historic 2018-19 season

Team Results:

  • Tied the school record for most wins in a season at 13 match wins
  • Tied the school record by recording 4 consecutive shutout wins in a season
  • First time in program history winning a League match

Individual Results:

  • Broke the school record for the most dual wins in a season with 15 wins
  • Named to Liberty League All-Academic Spring 2019 team
  • Played 5th singles and 3rd doubles
  • Appeared in 22 singles matches and 21 doubles matches
